# Horizon Outdoors — Product FAQ

## Alpine Pro Backpack (65L)

The Alpine Pro is our flagship expedition backpack designed for multi-day treks. It features an adjustable torso length system, a removable top lid that converts to a summit pack, and rain cover built into the base compartment. Weight: 1.8kg. Available in Slate Grey and Forest Green. Price: $289.

The harness system uses our proprietary AirFlow back panel which maintains ventilation even under heavy loads. Load capacity is rated to 25kg. The hip belt includes zippered pockets sized for phones up to 6.7 inches.

## Ridgeline Waterproof Jacket

The Ridgeline uses a 3-layer waterproof breathable membrane rated at 20,000mm hydrostatic head and 15,000g/m² MVTR. All seams are fully taped. The hood is helmet-compatible with a wire brim for shape retention in high winds. Weight: 420g. Available in Midnight, Olive, and Burnt Orange. Price: $349.

Care instructions: Machine wash cold with technical wash detergent. Do not use fabric softener. Tumble dry low to reactivate DWR coating. Reapply DWR spray after every 20 washes or when water stops beading on the surface.

## Summit Trekking Poles (Pair)

Carbon fibre construction with tungsten carbide tips. Three-section telescopic design collapses from 135cm to 38cm. Features our QuickLock mechanism rated to 15kg of downward force. Each pole weighs 195g. Includes rubber tips for hard surfaces and snow baskets. Price: $129.

The QuickLock mechanism should be cleaned and dried after use in muddy or sandy conditions. If the lock becomes stiff, loosen the expander nut inside the bottom section and clean with warm water.

## TrailRest Sleeping Bag (-5°C)

Synthetic insulation rated to -5°C comfort, -10°C lower limit. Features a contoured hood with drawcord, internal pocket for valuables, and a two-way zip with anti-snag design. Pack size: 22cm × 38cm. Weight: 1.2kg. Available in Midnight and Olive. Price: $199.

Storage: Always store uncompressed in the included cotton storage sack, not the stuff sack. Prolonged compression degrades insulation loft and reduces thermal performance.

## BaseLayer Merino Collection

100% New Zealand merino wool, 185gsm weight. Available as crew neck, half-zip, and leggings. Naturally antimicrobial — stays fresh for multiple days without washing. Temperature regulating from -10°C to 20°C. Machine washable on wool cycle. Price: $89 (crew), $99 (half-zip), $79 (leggings).
