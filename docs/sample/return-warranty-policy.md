# Horizon Outdoors — Return & Warranty Policy

## Standard Returns

All items may be returned within 30 days of the delivery date. Items must be unused, unworn, and in their original packaging with all tags attached. A valid proof of purchase (order confirmation email or receipt) is required.

Return shipping is free for defective items. For change-of-mind returns, a flat rate of $12.90 applies, which is deducted from the refund.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item at our warehouse. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

## Exchanges

Exchanges are available for different sizes or colours of the same item at no additional cost. To request an exchange, contact our customer experience team with your order number. Exchange shipping is free in both directions.

If the desired size or colour is out of stock, we will process a full refund and notify you when the item is back in stock.

## Gift Returns

Items purchased as gifts can be returned with a gift receipt for store credit. Store credit is issued as a digital gift card sent to the recipient's email address. Store credit does not expire.

## Product Warranty

All Horizon Outdoors products carry a 12-month warranty from the date of purchase covering manufacturing defects. This warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, misuse, or damage caused by improper care.

To make a warranty claim, contact our customer experience team with your order number and photos of the defect. Approved claims receive either a replacement or full refund at the customer's choice.

Warranty claims do not require the original packaging. Proof of purchase is required.

## Items That Cannot Be Returned

The following items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged:
- Items marked as "Final Sale" or purchased during clearance events
- Personalised or custom-engraved items
- Underwear and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
- Items that have been altered or modified

## International Returns

International customers are responsible for return shipping costs and any applicable customs duties. Refunds for international orders are processed in the original purchase currency. Please allow 10-14 business days for international refund processing.
